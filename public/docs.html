Dexil Token Generation Platform

Welcome to Dexil, Dexil empowers anyone to effortlessly create and deploy secure ERC‑20 tokens on Ethereum. We've maintained our robust auto‑burn and liquidity tools, now with a fresh identity and improved experience.

How It Works

1

Connect your wallet

Use MetaMask, Trust Wallet, or any Web3-enabled wallet to connect to the platform

2

Fill in token details

Configure your token parameters: name, symbol, supply, taxes, and auto-burn settings

3

Submit and deploy to Ethereum

Review your settings and deploy your token with one click

4

Get verified on Etherscan

Our system automatically submits your contract for verification

5

Add liquidity

Provide initial liquidity with ETH and your tokens (LP tokens are auto-burned)

6

Go live on trading platforms

Your token is instantly available on DEXs and trackers

Secure Auto-Burn Feature

Dexil automatically burns a portion of your token supply based on pre-set conditions, during or right after deployment. This ensures immutable supply control and builds community trust.

Creating contracts while we ensure burn

Ensures that the deployer burns his liquidity once trading is opened

Immutable Burn

Once burned, tokens are permanently removed from circulation with no possibility of reversal.

Price Impact

Reduce supply to increase scarcity and potential value appreciation.

Platform Requirements

  • Web3 wallet: Currently MetaMask, we will integrate with other wallets such as Trust Wallet, Coinbase Wallet, or WalletConnect-compatible wallet
  • ETH balance: Minimum 0.02 ETH for service fee + gas costs
  • Browser: Latest version of Chrome with JavaScript enabled and metamask extention installed
  • Review: Carefully review all settings before final deployment

  • Access to token management dashboard

Token Parameters Explained

Parameter Description Constraints
Token Name Full name of your token (e.g., "Dexil Token") 3-30 characters
Token Symbol Ticker symbol (e.g., "DXL") 3-6 uppercase characters
Total Supply Initial token quantity minted to deployer 1M - 1B tokens (18 decimals)
Buy Tax Transaction tax applied to purchases 0-15% per transaction
Sell Tax Transaction tax applied to sales 0-15% per transaction

Security Architecture

Audited Smart Contracts

All deployment templates undergo rigorous security checks:

  • Static analysis with Slither and MythX
  • Automated formal verification
  • Third-party audits by Halborn Security

LP Protection Mechanism

Liquidity Pool tokens are automatically sent to dead address (0x00..dead) using our proprietary BurnRouter system, preventing rug pulls.

Ownership Controls

Optional multi-signature requirements for critical functions and ability to renounce ownership after deployment.

Gas Optimization

Our deployment contracts use cutting-edge optimizations to reduce your gas costs:

  • Bytecode Compression: Huff assembly for critical functions reduces contract size
  • Batch Operations: Single-transaction deployment flow minimizes interactions
  • Gas Refund Optimization: Maximize storage slot clearing refunds

Typical deployment costs: 650k-850k gas (≈0.006-0.008 ETH at 50 gwei) - 30-40% cheaper than manual deployment
